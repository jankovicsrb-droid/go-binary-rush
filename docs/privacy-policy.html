PRIVACY POLICY

Go Binary Rush  ·  Effective date: May 10, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 10, 2026

OVERVIEW

Go Binary Rush is a local puzzle game. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no account system, no server, no analytics, and no advertising.

DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE

The app stores the following data locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

  • Player callsign (name entered on first launch)
  • High scores and streak counters for each game mode
  • Daily challenge completion status and streak
  • Achievement and difficulty tier progress

Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data.

INTERNET ACCESS

The app declares the INTERNET permission solely to download the JetBrains Mono font from Google Fonts CDN on first launch. No personal or gameplay data is transmitted during this request. After the font is cached locally, the app operates fully offline.

For Google's data practices regarding their services, see the Google Privacy Policy.

THIRD-PARTY SDKS AND SERVICES

The app does not integrate any advertising networks (e.g. AdMob), crash reporting tools (e.g. Firebase Crashlytics), or analytics SDKs (e.g. Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel).

The only third-party component is the Google Fonts library, used exclusively for font delivery as described above.

CHILDREN

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. No data is collected from any user regardless of age.

DATA SAFETY (GOOGLE PLAY)

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements:

  • No personal data is collected or shared
  • No data is transmitted to any server
  • No advertising ID is accessed
  • No analytics or tracking is performed

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this URL with an updated effective date. Since the app does not collect contact information, users are encouraged to check this page periodically.
