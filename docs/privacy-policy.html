PRIVACY POLICY

Go Binary Rush  ·  Last updated: 2026-05-10

OVERVIEW

Go Binary Rush does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app is a local puzzle game — no account, no server, no analytics.

DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE

The app stores the following data locally using Android's SharedPreferences:

  • Player callsign (name you enter on first launch)
  • High scores and streak counters for each game mode
  • Daily challenge completion status and streak
  • Achievement and progress counters
  • Difficulty tier progress

This data is stored only on your device. It is never transmitted to any server or shared with any third party. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

INTERNET ACCESS

The app requests the INTERNET permission solely to download the JetBrains Mono font from Google Fonts CDN on first launch. No personal or gameplay data is transmitted. After the font is cached, the app works fully offline. For Google's data practices regarding their CDN, see Google's Privacy Policy.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The app does not use advertising networks, crash reporting services, or analytics SDKs. The only third-party component is the Google Fonts library (font delivery only, described above).

CHILDREN

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

Any changes to this policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date. The app does not collect contact information, so users are encouraged to check this page periodically.
